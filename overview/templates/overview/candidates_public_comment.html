Written Comments

Data source

We pulled about 8,000 comments from the Council Record Website spanning from early 2016 through October 2023. This is not a complete record of all public comment given in that time period, as that website does not capture in-person comments, and there are about 150 instances where individuals were either not identified or were grouped together as "sundry comments".

We've provided comments made by council candidates below.

Data patterns

Although the data we have is a subset of all comments, there are some general patterns that would likely hold for in-person and "sundry" comments as well:

  • Most people do not send in comments. Even if all of the ~8,000 comments were from different individuals, over 115,000 people live in Cambridge - and more certainly moved in and out over the time period.
  • It's common to only comment once: about 1/3 of the comments we pulled had unique senders.
  • The majority of comments come from repeat commenters.
  • There are a number of "super commenters" who've commented dozens or even over a hundred times. Sometimes these people are commenting for a group they are a part of, sometimes they are commenting on their own.
